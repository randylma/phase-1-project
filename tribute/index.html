Kobe Bryant

The greatest Laker of all time

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant life:

  • 1978 - Kobe was born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1992 - 1996 - He attended Lower Merion High School.
  • 1996 - 1999 - He emerged as a premier guard in the league.
  • 1999 - 2002 - He won three consecutive championships with the Lakers.
  • 2002 - 2007 - He became one of the best players in the NBA.
  • 2007 - 2010 - He won two more championships with the Lakers in 2009 and 2010.
  • 2010 - 2016 - He suffered a number of serious injuries.
  • 2016 - He retired from the NBA in 2016.
  • 2020 - Kobe died in a helicopter crash on January 26, 2020.
